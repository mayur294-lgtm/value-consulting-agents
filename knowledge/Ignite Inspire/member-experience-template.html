Fundamental Problems of the Current Digital Experience

Problem 1

Channel Fragmentation

Members/customers experience different capabilities and interfaces across OLB, mobile, and branch. Starting a process in one channel often requires restarting in another.

Problem 2

Incomplete Digital Journeys

Key processes like lending and dispute resolution begin digitally but require branch visits or phone calls to complete, breaking the member/customer experience.

Problem 3

No Personalization

Every member/customer sees the same generic experience regardless of their segment, behavior, life stage, or relationship depth with the institution.

Problem 4

Data Not Leveraged

Rich transaction and behavioral data exists but is not used for insights, recommendations, or proactive financial wellness guidance.
