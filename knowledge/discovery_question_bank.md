# Discovery Question Bank

A comprehensive set of discovery questions organized by domain, journey, and value lever. Use these to guide discovery workshops, stakeholder interviews, and data collection sessions.

---

## General Business Context

### Strategic Direction
1. What are your organization's top 3-5 strategic priorities for the next 2-3 years?
2. How does digital banking fit into your overall strategy?
3. What is your target customer segment mix (retail, SME, wealth, corporate)?
4. Are you planning geographic expansion or new market entry?
5. What is your M&A outlook (acquisitions, divestitures)?

### Financial Performance
1. What is your current cost-to-income ratio? What is the target?
2. What is your revenue growth target for the next 3 years?
3. What is the breakdown of fee vs. interest income?
4. What is your budget for digital transformation initiatives?
5. What is your typical investment approval threshold and process?

### Competitive Position
1. Who are your top 3 competitors?
2. How do you differentiate from them today?
3. What are you losing deals/customers on?
4. Are there new entrants (fintechs, neobanks) affecting your market share?
5. What is your current NPS vs. competitors?

## Customer Acquisition & Onboarding

### Volume & Conversion
1. How many new customers do you acquire per month/year?
2. What is your current digital acquisition rate (%)?
3. What is your application-to-funded account conversion rate?
4. At what stages do you see the most drop-off?
5. What is the typical time from application to funded account?

### Channel Mix
1. What % of applications come through digital vs. branch vs. call center?
2. What is the conversion rate by channel?
3. Do you have different products available by channel?
4. What is the cost of acquisition by channel?

### Process Efficiency
1. What is your current straight-through processing (STP) rate?
2. What are the top reasons for manual intervention?
3. How many FTEs are involved in onboarding operations?
4. What is the average time spent per application (by role)?
5. What rework or error rates do you experience?

### KYC/AML
1. What is your KYC completion rate?
2. What is the average time for identity verification?
3. What % of applications require manual AML review?
4. What are your false positive rates for AML screening?
5. What compliance technology do you use (Jumio, Onfido, etc.)?

### Benchmark Questions
1. What % of applicants complete onboarding within 24 hours?
2. What is your digital account opening leakage rate?
3. How does your onboarding time compare to competitors?

## Lending & Loan Origination

### Volume & Performance
1. What is your annual loan origination volume (by product)?
2. What is your average loan size?
3. What is your approval rate by channel?
4. What is your time to decision? Time to disbursement?
5. What is your current pull-through rate (approved â†’ funded)?

### Process
1. What is your STP rate for loan applications?
2. What % of applications require manual underwriting?
3. How many touches/handoffs occur in the loan process?
4. What is the average cost per loan originated?
5. What document collection challenges do you face?

### Portfolio Quality
1. What is your NPL (non-performing loan) ratio?
2. What is your early delinquency rate (30/60/90 DPD)?
3. What is your loan loss provision rate?
4. How does digital channel loan quality compare to branch?

### Technology & Integration
1. What LOS (loan origination system) do you use?
2. Is it integrated with your core banking system?
3. How do you handle credit decisioning? What bureau(s)?
4. What is the typical change request timeline for LOS updates?

## Customer Servicing

### Channel Usage
1. What % of transactions are digital vs. branch vs. call center?
2. What is your mobile app adoption rate?
3. What is your digital active customer rate?
4. What are your top 10 servicing requests by volume?
5. Which requests are available digitally vs. require human assistance?

### Operational Efficiency
1. What is your average call center handle time?
2. What is your first-call resolution rate?
3. How many FTEs support customer service operations?
4. What is the cost per interaction by channel?
5. What is your IVR containment rate?

### Self-Service Capabilities
1. Can customers update their own profile information online?
2. Can customers manage cards (activate, block, set limits) digitally?
3. Can customers initiate disputes online?
4. What self-service capabilities are missing?

### Experience Metrics
1. What is your customer NPS by segment?
2. What are your top customer complaints?
3. What is your app store rating?
4. What is your customer effort score?

## Wealth Management Specific

### Client Portfolio
1. How many wealth clients do you serve?
2. What is your total AUM?
3. What is your average AUM per client?
4. How is your client base segmented (mass affluent, HNW, UHNW)?
5. What is your client growth rate? Attrition rate?

### Advisor Productivity
1. How many RMs/advisors do you have?
2. What is the average client-to-advisor ratio?
3. How much time do advisors spend on admin vs. client-facing?
4. What is the average NNA (net new assets) per advisor per year?
5. How long does client meeting prep take?

### Onboarding
1. How long does wealth onboarding take end-to-end?
2. What % is paper-based vs. digital?
3. What is your onboarding drop-off rate?
4. How many hours does the RM spend per onboarding?
5. What are the major bottlenecks in onboarding?

### Reporting & Review
1. How often do clients receive portfolio reports?
2. How long does report preparation take?
3. How many systems does the RM need to access for a client review?
4. Do clients have self-service access to their portfolio?
5. What is the digital engagement rate for wealth clients?

### Investment Operations
1. Can clients place trades digitally?
2. What is the trade execution process?
3. How are investment proposals generated?
4. What discretionary vs. advisory vs. execution-only mix do you have?

## Value Lever Specific Questions

### Revenue Generation Levers

**Customer Acquisition:**
- What is the revenue per new customer in Year 1?
- What is the customer lifetime value?
- What would a 10% improvement in conversion be worth?

**Cross-Sell/Upsell:**
- What is your products-per-customer ratio?
- What is your cross-sell conversion rate?
- What is the average revenue per additional product?

**Loan Volume:**
- What is your average loan margin?
- What is your revenue per funded loan?
- What is the impact of faster disbursement?

### Cost Reduction Levers

**Operational Efficiency:**
- What is your fully-loaded FTE cost (by role)?
- How many FTEs could be redeployed with automation?
- What is the cost per transaction/interaction by channel?

**Branch Optimization:**
- What is your average branch operating cost?
- What % of branch transactions could migrate to digital?
- What branch consolidation plans do you have?

**IT Cost Reduction:**
- What legacy systems could be retired?
- What is the maintenance cost of current platforms?
- What efficiency could be gained with modern architecture?

### Risk & Compliance Levers

**Fraud Reduction:**
- What is your current fraud loss rate?
- What fraud prevention technology do you use?
- What is your false positive rate?

**Compliance Efficiency:**
- What is your regulatory compliance cost?
- What manual compliance processes exist?
- What regulatory changes are upcoming?

## Data Collection Templates

### Essential Metrics to Collect

**Volume Data:**
- Monthly new customers
- Monthly applications by product
- Monthly transactions by type
- Active customers by channel

**Conversion/Performance Data:**
- Application conversion rates by stage
- Drop-off rates by channel
- STP rates by product
- Time-to-X metrics (decision, funding, etc.)

**Cost Data:**
- FTE counts by function
- Fully-loaded FTE costs
- Cost per transaction by channel
- Vendor/licensing costs

**Experience Data:**
- NPS scores
- App ratings
- Complaint volumes
- Customer effort scores

- What does success look like?
- What are the non-negotiables?

- What are the top product gaps?
- What features are customers asking for?
- What competitive pressure exists?

- Where are the biggest inefficiencies?
- What manual processes cause delays?
- What would you automate first?
