# Investing Domain Use Cases

## Acquire Stage

### UC-INV-01: Digital Investment Account Opening
**Description:** End-to-end digital account opening with suitability assessment, risk profiling, KYC/AML, and account funding in a single flow.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Onboarding, Flow Foundation, Platform Identity, Grand Central
**Value:** Reduces account opening cost by 60-80%, increases completion rate by 20-30%

### UC-INV-02: Banking-to-Investing Cross-Sell
**Description:** Contextual prompts within the banking app identify eligible clients and guide them to start investing — pre-populated from banking profile, zero re-entry.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Engage (NBA engine), Digital Banking, Digital Onboarding, Digital Invest
**Value:** +2-5% banking-to-investing conversion, captures investment wallet in-house

### UC-INV-03: Advisor-Assisted Account Opening (Hybrid)
**Description:** Advisor initiates account opening on behalf of client, client completes suitability and signatures digitally. Best of both worlds.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Assist (Advisor workspace), Digital Onboarding, Flow Foundation
**Value:** Reduces advisor onboarding time by 40-60%, maintains personal touch

## Activate Stage

### UC-INV-04: Guided First Investment
**Description:** Post-funding journey that guides new investors to their first trade or portfolio setup based on their suitability profile and goals.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Invest, Digital Engage (activation campaigns)
**Value:** +20-30% first-trade rate within 30 days

### UC-INV-05: Robo-Advisory Enrollment
**Description:** Self-directed investors can enroll in automated portfolio management — goal setting, portfolio preview, fee disclosure, one-click enrollment.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Invest (Robo module), Flow Foundation
**Value:** 5-10% self-directed → robo conversion, incremental fee revenue

### UC-INV-06: Recurring Investment Setup
**Description:** Prompted recurring investment setup (weekly/monthly auto-invest) during activation or as ongoing engagement prompt.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Invest, Digital Engage
**Value:** +15-25% recurring investment adoption, drives AUM growth

## Expand Stage

### UC-INV-07: ACAT Transfer-In (Asset Consolidation)
**Description:** Digital flow for transferring assets from other brokerages — firm lookup, guided form, real-time status tracking.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Onboarding (ACAT flow), Grand Central (clearing integration), Digital Engage (proactive prompts)
**Value:** +10-20% ACAT completion rate, $50K-$200K AUM per transfer

### UC-INV-08: Additional Account Opening (IRA, Joint, Custodial)
**Description:** Existing investor opens additional account types with minimal re-entry — profile carry-forward, streamlined suitability.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Onboarding, Flow Foundation
**Value:** Increases accounts per investor, captures tax-advantaged assets

### UC-INV-09: Self-Directed to Advisory Upgrade
**Description:** Proactive identification of investors whose AUM/complexity warrants advisory services. Seamless transition without re-paperwork.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Engage (trigger campaigns), Digital Assist (advisor workspace), Data Foundations (propensity models)
**Value:** Captures graduating investors before they leave for competitors, revenue uplift from advisory fees

## Retain Stage

### UC-INV-10: Market Volatility Communication
**Description:** Automated, segmented communication during market events — personalized to portfolio impact, risk profile, and investor type.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Engage (campaign orchestration), Data Foundations (segmentation)
**Value:** -30-50% support surge during volatility, +1-3% retention improvement

### UC-INV-11: Portfolio Review & Rebalancing
**Description:** Automated portfolio drift detection, rebalancing recommendations, and one-click execution.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Invest (rebalancing engine), Digital Engage (review prompts)
**Value:** Reduced portfolio drift, improved investor outcomes, reduced advisor burden

### UC-INV-12: Investor Self-Service Hub
**Description:** Self-service for account maintenance, beneficiary updates, tax documents, address changes, statement access.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Invest (self-service), Digital Banking (unified self-service)
**Value:** -20-40% call deflection on routine investing queries

### UC-INV-13: Advisor Escalation with Context
**Description:** Seamless handoff from self-service to advisor with full context — portfolio, activity history, recent interactions.
**Backbase Products:** Digital Assist (advisor workspace with Client 360), Digital Invest
**Value:** Higher advisor satisfaction, faster resolution, better investor experience
