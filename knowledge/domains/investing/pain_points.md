# Investing Domain Pain Points

## Investor Pain Points (Client-Facing)

### Onboarding & Account Opening

| Pain Point | Impact | Frequency | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Complex suitability questionnaires deter completion | High abandonment, lost prospects | Very Common | Flow Foundation (guided, conversational questionnaire), Digital Onboarding (smart forms) |
| Multiple days to open and fund an account | Prospect goes to competitor with instant access | Common | Digital Onboarding (same-day STP), Grand Central (instant ACH/funding connectors) |
| No visibility on application status | Anxiety, support calls, abandonment | Very Common | Digital Onboarding (real-time status tracking), Digital Engage (status notifications) |
| Separate account opening per account type (IRA, taxable, joint) | Friction for multi-account investors | Common | Flow Foundation (multi-account orchestration in single journey) |
| Paper-based processes for certain account types | Days of delay, poor experience for digital-native investors | Common (especially bank-led) | Digital Onboarding (end-to-end digital with eSignature, eConsent) |
| Difficult ACAT transfer-in process | Abandonment of asset consolidation, assets stay at competitor | Common | Digital Onboarding (guided ACAT flow), Grand Central (clearing firm integration) |

### Investing Experience

| Pain Point | Impact | Frequency | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Fragmented banking and investing experience | Investor manages money across disconnected platforms | Very Common (bank-led) | Digital Banking + Digital Invest (unified dashboard) |
| No guidance for first-time investors | Paralysis, zero activation, account sits unfunded | Common | Digital Invest (robo-advisory, goal-based investing), Digital Engage (education journeys) |
| Limited self-service for account changes | Calls/visits for address changes, beneficiary updates, account transfers | Common | Digital Invest (self-service hub), Digital Banking (unified self-service) |

| No personalized investment insights | Generic experience regardless of portfolio or goals | Common | Digital Engage (personalized content, NBA), Data Foundations (investor segmentation) |
| Opaque fees and performance reporting | Distrust, switching to transparent competitors | Common | Digital Invest (fee transparency, performance attribution reporting) |

### Market Events & Retention

| Pain Point | Impact | Frequency | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|-----------|-------------------|

## Provider Pain Points (Internal/Operational)

### Business Challenges

| Pain Point | Impact | Root Cause | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Banking clients investing elsewhere (Schwab, Fidelity, Vanguard) | Revenue leakage — investment wallet goes to competitors | No integrated investing proposition, no cross-sell engine | Digital Invest + Digital Engage (banking-to-investing pipeline with NBA) |
| Low conversion from account opening to first trade | Funded but inactive accounts = zero revenue | Poor activation journey, no guidance | Digital Engage (activation campaigns, first-trade nudges), Digital Invest (guided investing) |
| High cost of advisor-assisted onboarding | Limits scalability, especially for smaller accounts | Manual processes, paper forms, compliance documentation | Digital Onboarding (self-service with advisor override), Flow Foundation |
| Cannot scale robo-advisory profitably | Cost per account too high at small AUM | Manual rebalancing, compliance overhead | Digital Invest (automated rebalancing), Data Foundations (automated suitability monitoring) |
| Losing younger investors to neobrokers | Demographic time bomb — not capturing next generation | Outdated UX, no fractional shares, no social features | Digital Invest (modern UX), Digital Banking (integrated experience) |

### Operational Challenges

| Pain Point | Impact | Root Cause | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Suitability documentation is manual/paper-based | Compliance risk, audit burden, slow onboarding | Legacy systems, regulatory complexity | Flow Foundation (suitability journey with audit trail), Digital Onboarding |
| Advisor spends too much time on admin | Less time for client-facing revenue activity | Swivel-chair across 5-10 systems | Digital Assist (unified advisor workspace) |
| No unified view of banking + investing client | Miss cross-sell opportunities, poor service | Siloed systems between banking and brokerage | Data Foundations (unified client 360), Digital Assist (advisor 360) |
| Compliance review bottleneck | Slow account opening, new product onboarding delays | Manual review processes, limited automation | Flow Foundation (automated compliance workflows), Grand Central (RegTech integration) |
| Multiple systems for different account types | Operational complexity, training burden, errors | Historical system acquisitions | Grand Central (unified integration layer), Digital Invest (single platform) |

### Technology Challenges

| Pain Point | Impact | Root Cause | Backbase Solution |
|------------|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Legacy brokerage platform cannot support modern UX | Poor investor experience, losing to neobrokers | 10-20 year old technology, vendor lock-in | Digital Invest (modern engagement layer on top of existing systems) |
| No API layer for partner/fintech integration | Cannot offer modern services (fractional shares, crypto) | Monolithic architecture | Grand Central (API management, Fintech Marketplace) |
| Long time-to-market for new investment products | Miss market trends, regulatory windows | Tight coupling between product and technology | Flow Foundation (low-code journey builder), Developer Platform |
| Cannot personalize at scale | One-size-fits-all experience despite diverse investor base | No data infrastructure for segmentation | Data Foundations (investor analytics), Digital Engage (personalization engine) |

## Pain Point Discovery Questions

Use these in discovery sessions to surface investing-specific pain points:

### For Business Leaders
1. What percentage of your banking clients also invest with you vs. competitors?
2. How many accounts are opened but never funded? Funded but never traded?
3. What's your average time from account application to first investment?
4. How do you currently handle suitability assessment — digital, paper, or hybrid?
5. What happens when a client's assets grow beyond self-directed into advisory territory?

### For Operations / Technology
6. How many systems does an advisor touch to onboard a new investment client?
7. What's your ACAT transfer-in completion rate and average time?
8. How do you handle rebalancing — automated or manual? At what frequency?
9. What compliance processes are still paper-based or manual?
10. How is your investing platform integrated with your banking platform (if at all)?

### For Client Experience
11. What's your app store rating for the investing app? Top complaints?
12. How do you communicate with investors during market volatility?
13. What self-service capabilities do investors have today (account changes, beneficiary updates, tax documents)?
14. How do first-time investors discover and engage with your investing offering?
15. What's the experience when a banking client wants to start investing — is it seamless or do they start over?
