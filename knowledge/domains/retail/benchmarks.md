## Journey-Specific Benchmarks

### Digital Onboarding Journey

**Before Digital Transformation (Typical):**
- 3+ days end-to-end process
- 60-90% digital abandonment
- 2-6 internal handoffs
- 30-60 min branch processing time
- 30-40% funded account rate

**After Backbase (Target):**
- <15 min end-to-end digital
- <50% digital abandonment
- 1-2 internal handoffs
- <20 min branch processing (assisted)
- >70% funded account rate

### Cards Management Journey

**Before Digital Transformation (Typical):**
- 0-10% digital card requests
- 20-30 min branch/CC processing
- 7-10 day card delivery
- No real-time blocking

**After Backbase (Target):**
- >50% digital card requests
- <2 min self-service
- Instant virtual card issuance
- Real-time card controls
