Executive Summary

Discovery interviews with leadership reveal significant friction in digital onboarding and customer servicing. Customer drop-off during document collection, 7+ day onboarding times, and simple requests handled by expensive call center channels indicate an estimated $2.4M annual value leakage across acquisition and servicing.

We recommend a two-step approach: a $50K validation phase (4 weeks) to confirm key assumptions, followed by Phase 1 investment of $800K for Digital Onboarding Excellence, which delivers standalone ROI of 90%.
