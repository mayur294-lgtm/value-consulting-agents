Executive Summary

Strategic overview and key recommendations

The Opportunity

Discovery interviews with Head of Retail, Operations Lead, and Digital Lead reveal significant friction across two critical customer journeys: Digital Onboarding and Customer Servicing.

Customer drop-off during document collection, 7+ day onboarding cycle times, and simple service requests routed through expensive call center channels indicate an estimated $2.4M annual value leakage across acquisition costs, servicing inefficiency, and operational overhead.

This report presents a detailed capability assessment, journey analysis, and phased transformation roadmap to capture this value opportunity through digital enablement.

D. Methodology Notes

Maturity Assessment

Capabilities scored on 1-5 scale: (1) Initial - ad-hoc, person-dependent; (2) Developing - some processes, inconsistent; (3) Defined - standardized, consistent; (4) Managed - measured, controlled; (5) Optimized - industry-leading, predictive.

ROI Calculation

NPV calculated at 10% discount rate over 5-year horizon. Benefits ramped at 50% Year 1, 75% Year 2, 100% Years 3-5. Conservative scenario applies 50% benefit realization; Base case 75%; Aspirational 100%.

Evidence Confidence

High = direct statement with specific numbers; Medium = clear implication or directional statement; Low = inference or interpretation required. All claims mapped to evidence IDs.
