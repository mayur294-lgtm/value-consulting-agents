## The Opportunity (Journey-Based)

### Current State: Broken Onboarding, Expensive Servicing

Discovery revealed two critical capability gaps costing the bank revenue and margin:

1. **Onboarding Journey is Broken**
   - Completion rate is "low" with significant drop-off at document stage
   - Cycle time exceeds 7 days in some cases
   - Digital investment hasn't scaled; teams use manual workarounds

2. **Servicing is Expensive**
   - Simple requests still handled by call center
   - No visibility into customer channel behavior
   - Digital self-service underutilized

### Benefits by Journey

| Journey | Revenue | Cost Reduction | Total (Annual) |

## What Would Change This Decision?

### Decision Becomes "No Go" If:

1. Current completion rate is already >55% (less headroom)
2. Servicing volume is <400K annually (smaller opportunity)
3. FTE cost per hour is <$15 (less savings potential)
4. Implementation costs exceed $12M (breakeven at risk)

### External Risks:

1. Regulatory changes to eKYC requirements
2. Core banking modernization conflicts
3. Organizational change resistance (workarounds prevalent)
